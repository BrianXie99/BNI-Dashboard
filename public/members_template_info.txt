Members Excel Template Format
==============================

Required Columns:
- Phone_ID: Unique phone identifier (e.g., "1234567890")
- Member_Number: Member number (e.g., "M001")
- Name: Full name of the member
- Industry: Business industry/category
- Master: Master member name (optional, can be empty)
- Join_Date: Date joined BNI (format: YYYY-MM-DD or Excel date)
- Status: Member status (ACTIVE or INACTIVE)

Example:
Phone_ID    | Member_Number | Name      | Industry   | Master     | Join_Date  | Status
1234567890  | M001          | John Doe  | Technology | Jane Smith | 2024-01-01 | ACTIVE
0987654321  | M002          | Jane Smith| Finance    |            | 2024-02-01 | ACTIVE

Notes:
- Phone_ID must be unique for each member
- Join_Date can be in YYYY-MM-DD format or Excel date serial number
- Master field is optional
- Status must be either "ACTIVE" or "INACTIVE"
